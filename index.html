One year...

Thirteen full moons have traced our skies, a year of truth behind our eyes. Through shifting light and shadowed lands, we held our hearts in steady hands.

You, my Moon, in silver grace, softly pull my soul in place. I, your Sun, in golden fire, burn for you with quiet desire.

We walked through storms, through doubt, through night, yet always found our way to light. For love like ours does not decay it deepens slowly, day by day.

A year has passed, yet still it seems like we are living inside dreams. For every rise and every fall, we choose again our hearts recall.

Thirteen moons, one sacred year, and still your glow draws me near. No fate more true, no thread more strong, two souls aligned where they belong.

So let the heavens spin above we move in timeless, endless love. The Sun and Moon, forever one, an eternal orbit just begun.
